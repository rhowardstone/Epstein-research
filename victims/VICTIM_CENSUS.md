## SECTION 5: VICTIM COMPENSATION AND RIGHTS

### 5A. Epstein Victims' Compensation Program (EVCP)
- Established May 29, 2020 ([EFTA00010224](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%208/EFTA00010224.pdf))
- Administered by Kenneth R. Feinberg and Camille S. Biros
- Eligibility: "individuals who allege they were sexually abused by Epstein"
- No statute of limitations bar: "An individual whose claim is time-barred... may participate"
- Prior settlements do not disqualify participation
- Maxwell defense attempted to subpoena EVCP administrator (quashed by court, [EFTA00010118](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%208/EFTA00010118.pdf))

### 5B. CVRA Violations
- The NPA "was affirmatively concealed from the victims" ([EFTA00021442](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%208/EFTA00021442.pdf))
- Judge found USAO violated Crime Victims' Rights Act (18 U.S.C. 3771) by failing to confer with victims before executing NPA ([EFTA00013359](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%208/EFTA00013359.pdf))
- DOJ OPR investigated prosecutor conduct in 2007-2008 NPA negotiations ([EFTA00011475](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%208/EFTA00011475.pdf))

### 5C. USVI Victim Fund
- V.I. Attorney General Denise George filed civil enforcement under CICO Act
- Settlement included access to FBI Victim Services and Child USA counseling ([EFTA00037535](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%208/EFTA00037535.pdf), [EFTA00037522](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%208/EFTA00037522.pdf))
- Estate agreed to "release of a portion of estate funds for the victims" ([EFTA00037535](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%208/EFTA00037535.pdf))

### Aggregate Assessment
The documentary record supports the following victim count ranges:

- **Florida operation (2002-2008)**: At minimum 35 identified victims, "well over 30" confirmed by FBI, likely 40-50+ given the recruitment pyramid structure where "Epstein paid hundreds of dollars to victim-recruiters for each additional girl."

- **Full scope (all locations, 1990s-2019)**: The USVI Attorney General's characterization of "hundreds" is the broadest official claim, supported by:
  - The computerized database of available girls maintained by the Epstein Enterprise
  - The masseuse list recovered from electronic devices
  - The geographic spread across 5+ locations over 20+ years
  - The recruitment pyramid model that continuously generated new victims
  - Air traffic controller observations of very young girls as late as 2018

- **Conservative documented minimum**: 35 (FBI Miami interviews) + "dozens" additional (SDNY) = **~60-80 individually identified victims** across both investigations.

- **Realistic total estimate**: Given the 20+ year duration, multiple locations, recruitment pyramid, and the USVI's "hundreds" characterization, the actual number of victims likely exceeds **100** and may approach or exceed **200**.

## KEY EVIDENTIARY GAPS

1. **Masseuse list contents**: The actual names on the list recovered from Epstein's devices have not been fully disclosed in the available documents.
2. **EVCP claim count**: The total number of claims filed with the Epstein Victims' Compensation Program is not stated in the available record.
3. **USVI database**: The "computerized list of underage girls" referenced in the GVI complaint has not been detailed in the available documents.
4. **International victims**: The UK-based victim reference ([EFTA00095305](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%209/EFTA00095305.pdf)) and Paris flight records suggest victims outside the US who may not appear in domestic counts.
5. **Post-NPA victims (2008-2019)**: The USVI allegation of abuse "as recently as 2018" implies victims from the decade after the Florida NPA who were never part of the original investigation.
