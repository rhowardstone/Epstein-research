# Kings, Royalty and World Leaders in the Epstein Files

## The Story

Between his release from a Florida jail in July 2009 and his arrest by the FBI in July 2019, Jeffrey Epstein maintained and in some cases expanded relationships with royalty, heads of state, and senior government officials across at least eight countries. The EFTA documents — 2.91 million pages released by the DOJ — show repeated efforts to broker access among politicians, royals, financiers, and state-adjacent actors. This was more than social climbing. It was infrastructure.

Epstein connected political figures to financial institutions. He connected JPMorgan's Jes Staley to Peter Mandelson, who was then UK Business Secretary. He connected Staley to Ehud Barak, the former Israeli Prime Minister. He connected Staley to Nicolas Sarkozy's inner circle at the Élysée Palace. He proposed connecting Sultan Ahmed bin Sulayem, who ran the world's largest port operator, to Barak. These introductions appear to have increased Epstein's value to major banking circles, especially at JPMorgan, by making him useful as a connector across political and financial networks.

The documents also show something else: the political relationships predated the conviction. FedEx invoices from 2002 show packages shipped from Epstein and Ghislaine Maxwell to Peter Mandelson at the House of Commons. Prince Andrew's emails with Maxwell date to 2001. But the released documents are sparse before mid-2009 and contain virtually no personal email before September 2007. The peak period of relationship-building (late 1990s through 2006) is largely invisible. What survives in volume is the *maintenance* of relationships established earlier — the post-conviction rebuilding of a network that should have been destroyed.

This report presents every connection chronologically, citing the source documents and crediting the journalists who reported each finding first.

## Before the Conviction (2001–2008)

### 2001–2002: Andrew, Maxwell, and Mandelson

The earliest documents involving royalty are seven emails between **Prince Andrew** and **Ghislaine Maxwell**, recovered from their devices by the FBI.

On **August 16, 2001**, Andrew wrote from Balmoral: "I am up here at Balmoral Summer Camp for the Royal Family...The Girls are completely shattered." He asked Maxwell: "Have you found me some new inappropriate friends?" The email was sent from `abx17@dial.pipex.com`, a UK ISP account listed in the FBI device search warrant. On **March 3, 2002**, Maxwell sent what prosecutors later identified in a Federal Rule of Evidence 404(b) letter ([EFTA00080134](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%209/EFTA00080134.pdf)) as the "2 legged sight seeing" email referencing "the Duke of York."

These emails were first reported by **NBC News** (Matt Lavietes, [December 23, 2025](https://www.nbcnews.com/news/us-news/andrew-mountbatten-windsor-appears-asked-ghislaine-maxwell-new-inappro-rcna250710)) and **ABC News** ([same day](https://abcnews.go.com/US/emails-believed-prince-andrew-ghislaine-maxwell-surface-latest/story?id=128662995)).

In that same period, Epstein and Maxwell were shipping packages to **Peter Mandelson** at Parliament. Two FedEx invoices from the Maxwell trial production document:

- **August 8, 2002:** 3.0 lbs, FedEx International Priority, from Jeffrey E. Epstein, 457 Madison Avenue, to Peter Mandelson, The Right Honorable Peter Mandelson, Member of Parliament, House of Commons, London. Delivered August 12 ([EFTA01317758](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2010/EFTA01317758.pdf)).
- **November 8, 2002:** 1.0 lbs, sender listed as "O. MAXWE[LL]" / Jeffrey E Epstein, same address. Delivered November 11 ([EFTA01313812](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2010/EFTA01313812.pdf)).

Also on the November invoice: a shipment from **Conrad Black** to Epstein. The contents of all packages are unknown.

Separately, **The Times** (Steven Swinford) reported that on **May 14, 2002**, **Tony Blair** met Epstein at Downing Street, where Blair discussed consulting "a wise religious man" and "the nature that religion plays in world conflicts." **LBC** ([March 2026](https://www.lbc.co.uk/article/mandelson-emailed-tony-blairs-chief-of-staff-safe-friend-epstein-5HjdWCy_2/)) later reported that Mandelson had emailed Blair's chief of staff Jonathan Powell: "Jeffrey is in London next week...he is safe (whatever that means)...Clinton is now doing a lot of travelling with him."

**What these documents do NOT show:** The scope of the pre-conviction relationships. The FedEx packages could contain anything — books, documents, gifts. The email record is sparse before mid-2009; the 2001–2002 materials survive only because they were on seized devices.

### 2008: Conviction and Barak

Epstein pleaded guilty in June 2008 and served 13 months. During **Passover 2008**, while arrangements were being made for his sentence, Epstein toured IDF military bases in Israel with **Ehud Barak** (then Defense Minister). First reported by **Haaretz** ([July 11, 2019](https://www.haaretz.com/israel-news/2019-07-11/ty-article/.premium/revealed-jeffrey-epstein-entered-million-dollar-partnership-with-ehud-barak-in-2015/0000017f-e376-d75c-a7ff-ffff11630000)), which also broke the Carbyne/Reporty investment partnership.

## The Rebuild (2009–2012)

### 2009: Immediate Post-Release

Epstein was released in July 2009, but the documents show the rebuilding was already underway before his release — and accelerated immediately after.

**June 5, 2009** (pre-release): Epstein emailed Jes Staley about Mandelson: "well for all intends and purposes peter mandelson is now deputy prime minister" ([EFTA02808649](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2012/EFTA02808649.pdf)).

**June 17, 2009** (pre-release): Epstein to Staley: "peter will be staying at 71st over weekend, do you want to organize either you, or you and Jamie, quietly, up to you." 71st Street is Epstein's Manhattan townhouse. "Jamie" is Jamie Dimon, JPMorgan's CEO.

**June 30, 2009** (pre-release): First documented email from **Sultan Ahmed bin Sulayem** (Chairman/CEO, DP World), the most extensively documented Gulf relationship in the files — over 5,000 documents spanning a decade. First reported by **Drop Site News** (Ryan Grim and Murtaza Hussain, [January 14, 2026](https://www.dropsitenews.com/p/jeffrey-epstein-united-arab-emirates-sultan-sulayem-dubai-dp-world)).

**August 2009:** A conference planning document listed **Tony Blair** alongside **George Mitchell**, **Ehud Barak**, Larry Summers, and Bloomberg as desired participants for intellectual gatherings Epstein intended to fund ([EFTA00773584](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%209/EFTA00773584.pdf)).

**September 22, 2009:** A contact invited Epstein to "a very private evening in honor of President **Sarkozy** and **Carla Bruni**" at $25,000 per couple, hosted by Stanley Shopkorn, and asked Epstein to recruit wealthy guests ([EFTA00773515](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%209/EFTA00773515.pdf)). Whether Epstein attended is not documented.

**November 29, 2009:** Epstein pitched Staley on a JPMorgan Middle East strategy: "think of the financial equivalent of aaramco.. jpm should be the oracle of middle east finance... **tony blair can lead that charge**" ([EFTA00742761](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%209/EFTA00742761.pdf)). ## The Pattern: Access Broker

Across the full decade, a consistent architecture emerges. Epstein did not merely collect powerful contacts — he **connected them to each other**, and specifically to financial institutions:

**The JPMorgan pipeline:**
- Epstein → **Mandelson** → Staley (Davos, Shanghai, Congo mining, Dodd-Frank)
- Epstein → **Blair** → Staley ("tony blair can lead that charge")
- Epstein → **Remmert/Sarkozy** → Staley ("Who is the woman helping Sarkozy?")
- Epstein → **Barak** → Staley (multiple confirmed meetings)
- Epstein → **Sultan bin Sulayem** → Staley (island meeting)

**The Rothschild pipeline:**
- Epstein → **Colom** → Sarkozy circle
- Epstein → **Colom** → Le Maire
- Epstein → **Ariane** → Barak → Sarkozy (January 2014 dinner proposed; unconfirmed)


**The Bannon pipeline (2018–2019):**
- Epstein → **Bannon** → Barak (confirmed meeting, February 2019)
- Bannon discussed Salvini/Grillo/Berlusconi meetings with Epstein (March 2018)
- Bannon floated Front National conference address; Epstein advised against

Each political figure provided Epstein with something different: Mandelson provided UK government and Chinese banking access. Sarkozy's circle provided French G20 policy intelligence. Barak provided Israeli defense/tech connections. Sultan bin Sulayem provided Gulf port and shipping infrastructure. In return, Epstein provided introductions into major banking circles, especially at JPMorgan — which appear to have helped sustain the environment in which his banking relationships survived.

Not every item in this report represents confirmed brokerage. Some entries show **confirmed direct coordination** (Mandelson/Staley/Davos, Barak/Sultan bin Sulayem meeting). Others show **proposed or attempted introductions** (Sultan of Brunei, Berlusconi, Sarkozy/Carla dinner invitation). Still others are **third-party descriptions or name-drops** (Nikolic reporting who he met at Davos, Brunel forwarding French election results). The distinction matters: a proposed meeting is not a completed one, and a name in an email is not a relationship.

**What this pattern does NOT prove:** Political introductions for financial institutions are not inherently unlawful. Many contacts occurred post-conviction but before the full scope of Epstein's crimes was publicly understood. The question the USVI litigation addressed was not whether introductions occurred, but whether JPMorgan continued the relationship specifically because of them.
