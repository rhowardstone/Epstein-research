---

## PURPOSE

The existing [BIOTECH_SCIENCE_NETWORK_INVESTIGATION](BIOTECH_SCIENCE_NETWORK_INVESTIGATION.md) covered approximately **12 scientists** in depth (Nowak, Chomsky, Hillis, Krauss, Church, Lloyd, Ito, Boyden, Marsh, Slavin, Gershenfeld, Seung). This audit searched the full corpus for **every scientist known to have Epstein connections** and identified **30+ additional individuals with significant document counts who were not previously investigated.**

The most significant gap: **Robert Trivers** (943 documents, FBI FinCEN investigation) was completely absent from all reports.

---

## METHODOLOGY

Every name was searched across `full_text_corpus.db` (1,380,937 documents, 2,731,796 pages, 4.79 GB). Document counts reflect unique EFTA numbers containing the search term. Context snippets were extracted for classification (direct email, calendar entry, contact list, news article, FBI document, etc.).
