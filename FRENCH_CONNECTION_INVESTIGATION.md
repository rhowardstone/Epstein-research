## Executive Summary

The EFTA corpus — the largest single release of Jeffrey Epstein documents, produced by the DOJ under a federal court order — contains extensive evidence of Epstein's operations in France spanning at least 2005 through 2019. This report consolidates that evidence into a single reference organized around six investigative threads:

1. **The Modeling Pipeline** — Jean-Luc Brunel, MC2 Model Management, Karin Models, and Daniel Siad operated a multinational recruitment network that funneled young women to Epstein through Paris, using visa dependency and financial control as coercion mechanisms.

2. **22 Avenue Foch** — Epstein's Paris apartment, held through SCI JEP (999/1,000 shares), served as an operational base. A September 2019 French police search recovered photographs of undressed young women, 30 videos, and a CD-ROM with 62 women's names.

3. **Jack Lang and Prytanee LLC** — Former French Culture Minister Jack Lang and his daughter Caroline maintained a relationship with Epstein from at least 2012 through January 2019. This included a joint $5.2M art investment vehicle (Prytanee LLC) registered in the US Virgin Islands, private flights to Marrakech, and Caroline Lang arranging a meeting between Epstein and Dominique Strauss-Kahn.

4. **Fabrice Aidan** — A UN diplomat who brokered meetings between Epstein and Gulf-state royalty, intervened in visa processes for unnamed individuals, and directed unnamed "guests" to Epstein's Paris and New York properties.

5. **Frederic Chaslin** — A French orchestra conductor who offered Epstein a 21-year-old philosophy student in Paris, defended Harvey Weinstein while referencing his own "similar situation in Santa Fe," and visited Epstein's Zorro Ranch.

6. **Financial Infrastructure** — Deutsche Bank wired Epstein's funds through Societe Generale and BNP Paribas to support French operations, including property maintenance, luxury purchases, and local payments.

All findings below are drawn directly from the EFTA corpus with specific document citations. Every EFTA number can be verified at [justice.gov/epstein](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/) or at [epstein-data.com](https://epstein-data.com/).
