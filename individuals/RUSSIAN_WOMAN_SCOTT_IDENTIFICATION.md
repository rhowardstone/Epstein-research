## PART A: The Russian/Eastern European Woman

### Key Biographical Details from [EFTA01792918](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2010/EFTA01792918.pdf)

The email is a 5-page emotional communication from a young woman to Jeffrey Epstein. The extracted text reveals:

2. **University**: "Westminster International University in [REDACTED]" -- This is almost certainly **Westminster International University in Tashkent (WIUT)**, Uzbekistan. It is the ONLY institution in the world with this exact name. WIUT was established in 2002 as a franchise of the University of Westminster (London) and teaches entirely in English. This strongly suggests the woman is **Uzbek** (or from the broader Central Asian region), not Russian per se, though she uses Russian internet services (foto.rambler.ru, which is common across the former Soviet Union).

3. **Shoplifting Arrest**: She describes a shoplifting incident at Macy's with a friend. Both took clothes from the men's department to the women's fitting rooms. 7. **Work Prospect**: "I will have my work from March at Ages Engineering Company. They took me and I can feed myself."

9. **Flight**: She had a ticket for February 11th to go home.

10. **Epstein's Response (Feb 8)**: Epstein replied: "bring all the info.. your arrest and court date.. your visa info." -- This suggests Epstein was prepared to help with her legal situation, potentially using his lawyer network.

### Analysis of the "Scott" Email

The language of [EFTA01793116](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2010/EFTA01793116.pdf) reveals critical information:

1. **Third-party relay**: The sender is NOT Scott himself. Someone (likely an assistant or intermediary) is relaying Scott's question to Epstein. This matches how Epstein's staff (Lesley Groff, Sarah Kellen, etc.) often relayed messages from guests.

2. **"Having his friend fly to [REDACTED] to keep him company at night"**: This is coded language for arranging female companionship. "His friend" being flown in suggests this is a woman, and "company at night" is self-explanatory.

3. **Access and means**: The "Scott" in question has the resources or connections to arrange private flights to St. Thomas / Little St. James. This is not a tech worker or mechanic.

4. **Epstein's location**: Scott is asking about Epstein's schedule in St. Thomas, suggesting Scott would be there as Epstein's guest or neighbor on the island.

5. **February 2011 timing**: This is during Epstein's post-conviction period (released from Florida custody in 2009, still on sex offender probation). Epstein was using his private island extensively during this period.

### Most Likely Candidate

Based on the available evidence, the "Scott" cannot be definitively identified from the databases searched. The key candidates have been eliminated:
- Scott Rothstein was in prison
- Scott Borgerson was not yet in Epstein's orbit
- Scott Denett and Scott Stambaugh were employees/workers, not guests
- Scott Stackman was a bank advisor

The "Scott" is most likely an **unidentified wealthy associate** whose full name has been effectively redacted across all available databases. The fact that he is referred to by first name only (no surname) in the email suggests familiarity -- the sender and Epstein both know exactly which "Scott" is being discussed without further identification.

Possible avenues for further identification:
- Flight logs for February 2011 to/from St. Thomas
- Epstein's calendars or scheduling documents for February 2011
- Visitor logs for Little St. James island
- The redacted sender name in [EFTA01793116](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2010/EFTA01793116.pdf) may provide a link to the intermediary who could identify "Scott"
