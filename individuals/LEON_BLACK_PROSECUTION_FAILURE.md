## TIMELINE

### May 2021: Maxwell Spinoff ([EFTA02731604](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2012/EFTA02731604.pdf)/[EFTA02731578](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2012/EFTA02731578.pdf))
SDNY AUSA to FBI CEHT: "We'd like to ask you to interview a witness that would be a spin-off from the Maxwell case, in order to decide whether we should open an investigation."

FBI interviews victim (who needed interpreter) on May 18 with attorney Jeanne Christensen.

AUSA requests Leon Black 302s. Internal email: "I'm not inclined to open based on the other victim, for a variety of reasons, but just want to make sure I'm doing my due diligence."

KEY: "Two victims from the Epstein investigation who were assaulted by Black and discussed it during their initial FBI interviews" already existed. The Wigdor client made at least THREE victims.

### Aug 2021: Defer to Post-Trial ([EFTA02731618](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2012/EFTA02731618.pdf))
SDNY: "[Supervisor] prefers we wait until after the trial to interview this new victim." AUSA: "I'd like to do that so it doesn't seem like we are just rebuffing the victim."

### Oct-Nov 2021: Manhattan DA Takes Over ([EFTA02731583](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2012/EFTA02731583.pdf)/623/628)
ADAs Alissa Wimmer and Vanessa Puzio (Manhattan DA) contact SDNY about their parallel Black investigation. ADA Leah Saxtein had prior contact.

### Jan 2022: SDNY Declines ([EFTA02731583](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2012/EFTA02731583.pdf))
"We don't have any current plans to interview [victim] again... If you are still pursuing an investigation encompassing both victims, we are not likely to open another investigation."

### Feb 2023: Fourth Victim ([EFTA02731587](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2012/EFTA02731587.pdf)/593)
Manhattan DA: "We have a new CW who has come forward and alleged sexual abuse by Leon Black. This CW was trafficked by Maxwell and Epstein."

SDNY response (June 2023): "I was hoping to try to evaluate whether we'd be inclined to investigate the conduct before circling back to her."

### Jul 2023: $62.5M Settlement ([EFTA02731648](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2012/EFTA02731648.pdf))
Christensen (Wigdor) to SDNY: "Leon Black paid 62.5 million to USVI - and this does not include payments to individual women - it's outrageous that criminal charges have not been brought. I've heard one lawyer represents ten women that he sexually assaulted."

### Mar 2024: Video Allegation ([EFTA02731515](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2012/EFTA02731515.pdf))
Christensen: "I strongly believe that there is at least one video circulating on some disgusting sex site with [victim]. [She] told me she was videotaped at a hotel... I know the names of at least two of the men who she had to have sex with during this incident - there were 6 men there and she had to be with 3 of them. One is a former friend of Leon Black's."

Victim was contacted by online gamer who recognized her from a video showing "one girl having sex with 3 men in a row."

### Mar 2024: Photos from Martha's Vineyard ([EFTA02731490](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2012/EFTA02731490.pdf)/501)
"JE at the side of the boat with a young woman with blond hair... Maxwell who took these four photos. They stayed overnight just one night. It is clearly Oak Bluffs harbor on MV."

Shared: 2012 journal (entirety + typed translation), "more than 20 people who wrote in her journal contemporaneously."

### Oct 2024: Brad Edwards Problem ([EFTA02731577](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%2012/EFTA02731577.pdf))
Wigdor call notes: "Black retained Edwards as part of a legal settlement of allegations... There have been other instances where a victim's attorney has been hired by the defendant."
