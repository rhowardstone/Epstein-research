# Ronald S. Lauder — Corpus Inventory

**Scope:** What the EFTA corpus shows (and does not show) about Ronald Lauder's documented contact with Jeffrey Epstein, separate from his broader public profile.

---

## 1. Who Ronald Lauder Is

Ronald S. Lauder (b. 1944) is the younger son of Estée and Joseph Lauder, heir to the Estée Lauder Companies fortune, former U.S. Ambassador to Austria (April 16, 1986 – October 27, 1987) under Reagan, former Deputy Assistant Secretary of Defense for European and NATO policy (appointed 1984) in the Reagan DoD, and since June 10, 2007 President of the **World Jewish Congress (WJC)**. He founded the **Neue Galerie** (NYC), which opened November 16, 2001, dedicated to early-20th-century German and Austrian art. He is a central Estée Lauder Companies board figure, Museum of Modern Art trustee, and documented political donor across multiple U.S. election cycles — including a publicly reported **$5 million donation to the pro-Trump super PAC MAGA Inc. in March 2025**, and credited by John Bolton's memoir as the "prominent businessman" who suggested to Trump in 2019 that the U.S. acquire Greenland (Lauder subsequently acquired commercial holdings in Greenland; a 2026 Guardian analysis noted the concept "may intersect with his own business interests"). His brother Leonard Lauder is Chairman Emeritus of Estée Lauder Companies. His daughters are **Aerin Lauder** (Zinterhofer) and **Jane Lauder**, who married **Kevin Warsh** in April 2002.

## 2. Executive Finding

The corpus shows Ronald Lauder's relationship with Jeffrey Epstein was an operational and financial partnership, not passive social overlap.

It documents:

1. **A sustained operational and financial partnership.** In 2014 Epstein personally created **Friends Ventures LLC** for Ronald Lauder and Leon Black as 50/50 co-owners of a $25M Kurt Schwitters painting, and Epstein remained the coordinator of its tax and partnership filings through at least April 2016, including a 2016 succession plan for Lauder to buy out Black's 50% at $12.5M on Black's death.
2. **Recurring direct communication and in-person contact.** A May 2017 email in which Epstein tells a Kuwaiti contact that Lauder had "just left my house"; a fully scheduled "LUNCH w/Ron Lauder" calendared on August 17, 2017 and propagated through multiple Epstein schedule copies; and a January 30, 2019 iMessage from a Manhattan contact to Epstein reporting that Lauder had been in Abidjan the previous day.
3. **Near-continuous presence on Peggy Siegal-curated Epstein social lists** from at least 2010 onward, including one list naming all three Lauder-family principals (Ronald, Leonard, and Jane Lauder-Warsh) on the same roster.

What the corpus **does not** show: any evidence that Lauder was aware of, complicit in, or informed about Epstein's trafficking activity; any Lauder Foundation / Estée Lauder Companies / Neue Galerie / World Jewish Congress corporate transaction with Epstein; or any Lauder presence on flight manifests, Little Saint James logistics, or victim-adjacent documents.

Bloomberg (Feb 6, 2026) and Artnet News (2026) first reported the Friends Ventures LLC structure itself. This report adds the operational Epstein–Wechsler–Turrin correspondence that documents Epstein's multi-year administrative role in the partnership — the layer not visible in the prior reporting — and situates it alongside the direct-communication and social-network evidence the corpus provides.

## 4. The Strongest Documented Operational Relationship: Friends Ventures LLC (2014–2016+)

This is the core documented Lauder–Epstein relationship in the corpus. It already appears in our own project reports — `art/ART_INVESTIGATION_COMPLETE.md` §3 and `art/ART_INVESTIGATION_WEB_RESEARCH.md` §8 — and has been independently reported by Bloomberg (Feb 6, 2026: "Epstein Set Up LLC for Lauder, Black to Hold $25 Million Artwork") and Artnet News (2026: "Leon Black and Ronald Lauder Joined Forces to Buy Masterpieces").

**What this shows.**

- Epstein did not merely introduce Lauder and Black — he **created the legal vehicle** (Friends Ventures LLC, 2014) to let them co-own a $25M painting, and he **remained the operational coordinator** for that vehicle's tax filings and partnership-accounting through at least April 2016.
- "RL" (Ronald Lauder) and "leon" (Leon Black) are the two principals in the Epstein–Wechsler–Turrin working correspondence. Epstein is making decisions about use-tax contributions, EIN filings, and partnership returns on their behalf.
- The 2016 presentation documents an explicit $12.5M succession-purchase plan between Lauder and Black's estate — financial planning coordinated through Epstein for a private transaction projected to execute decades in the future.

**What this does NOT show.**

- Any evidence of Lauder's knowledge of or involvement in Epstein's trafficking activity.
- Any kickback, sham transaction, or below-market pricing.
- Any connection between Friends Ventures and Lauder family institutions — this is a personal, not an institutional, partnership.

It is, however, evidence that Ronald Lauder's relationship with Epstein included a **documented, sustained, operational financial partnership** coordinated by Epstein personally over multiple years — not merely social contact.

**Institutional overlap.** Lauder and Black both served as trustees of the **Museum of Modern Art** during this period. Black was MoMA Board Chairman; he announced on March 26, 2021 that he would not seek re-election, his chairmanship ended July 1, 2021, and he remains a MoMA trustee. Lauder has remained a MoMA trustee throughout.

**Reliability:** Tier 1 (primary Epstein production documents; multiple corroborating internal threads; independently reported in Bloomberg and Artnet).

> From Al-Rasheed (May 14, 11:27 PM): "very well many thnx and here is a refrence: KUNA : Yemeni FM: We are ready to go back to peace talks — Politics — 05/05/2017 … Al-Mekhlafi said they went 'to our brotherly country Kuwait' which hosted the meeting for four months last year. >
> From Epstein (Sun May 14, 9:35 PM): "much to do around the trump trip. I hope you are enjoying."

**What this shows.** In a direct, non-public email exchange, Epstein:

1. Confirms Ronald Lauder had been physically present at his house — "he just left my house when you arrived" — immediately before a discussion of Kuwait's Yemen peace-talk role. This is primary-source evidence of Lauder visiting Epstein in May 2017.
2. Describes Lauder as "the key for you" in a thread focused on Kuwait's Gulf-diplomatic effort, while forwarding a *Jerusalem Post* op-ed critical of Lauder's public position on Abbas/Palestinian moderation.
3. Discusses the **Trump trip** ("trump trip") in the same thread — Trump's May 20–23, 2017 Riyadh visit, his first foreign trip as president.

**What this does NOT show.** The phrase "he is the key for you" is ambiguous on its own terms. It could mean Epstein is offering Lauder as a diplomatic intermediary, or telling Al-Rasheed that Lauder is the obstacle he needs to work around on the Abbas question (the op-ed linked in the same message is critical of Lauder's position), or something else that cannot be recovered from a single email. What the document fixes as established is (a) Lauder's physical visit to Epstein's house, (b) Epstein subsequently characterizing Lauder's policy posture as directly relevant to a Kuwaiti contact's Gulf-diplomatic objective, and (c) the contemporaneity with the Trump Riyadh trip. Whether Lauder was aware he was being discussed, or took any resulting action, is not in this document.

**Reliability:** Tier 1 (primary EFTA-production email).

## 10. Conflict Notes

- **Public profile vs. corpus profile.** Lauder's public profile is almost entirely philanthropic (WJC, Neue Galerie, Estée Lauder Companies board) with occasional political-donor and informal-Trump-adviser visibility. The corpus shows additional layers: (a) an Epstein-structured personal financial partnership (Friends Ventures LLC with Leon Black, 2014 onward, §4), (b) direct in-person and correspondence contact in 2017, and (c) near-continuous presence on Peggy Siegal-curated Epstein social events from at least 2010 onward. These are not inconsistent with the philanthropic profile — they can all be true — but a confirmation review that treats Lauder purely as a "philanthropic father-in-law" is working from the public profile only.
- **"Overcounted" corrections in prior reports.** Earlier project reports referenced "900+" Lauder document mentions. The Feb 12, 2026 art-investigation audit reconciled this to **463 deduped documents**, with the 900+ figure reflecting Estée Lauder brand hits mixed in. This is a methodology note, not a substantive correction to Lauder's findings.
- **No known Lauder statement on Epstein.** We are not aware of Ronald Lauder having made any public statement about his relationship with Epstein since the Feb 2026 Bloomberg and Artnet reporting on Friends Ventures LLC. If such a statement exists and characterizes the Epstein relationship as limited to one-time acquaintance, it would be contradicted by: the Aug 17, 2017 lunch documentation, the multi-year Friends Ventures operational correspondence (2014–2016+), and the May 2017 "just left my house" email. Any lighter characterization (mutual social circles, art-world overlap) is consistent with the corpus.

1. **Friends Ventures LLC — current status.** Does the LLC still exist? What happened to the Schwitters after Black's 2021 MoMA exit and Epstein's 2019 death? Has the $12.5M succession-purchase been triggered or restructured? Partnership tax returns are potentially obtainable by Senate Finance.

Cross-searching "RL" alongside Wechsler/Turrin would extend this.

4. **Scope of the Aug 17, 2017 lunch.** What was discussed? Any notes in subsequent Epstein correspondence, calendar follow-ups, or forwarded items from that day?
5. **"He just left my house."** Which residence — East 71st Street, Zorro Ranch, Paris? What visit pattern does this imply for May 2017?
6. **World Jewish Congress.** No WJC document in this corpus — but WJC-side correspondence is outside scope and would require a separate inquiry.
7. **Estée Lauder Companies board governance.** Any Lauder-family disclosure, recusal, or internal flag related to Epstein contact? Not in this corpus.
